Key Values of UX

Variables Description
Brand Attributes Unique characteristics and qualities associated with a brand, influencing design elements, tone of voice, and visual identity.
User Profiles Fictional representations of different user types, including demographics, behaviors, motivations, and goals.
Goals Desired outcomes or achievements that users aim to accomplish when interacting with a product or service.
Site Audit Evaluation of an existing website or digital product to assess performance, usability, and adherence to design principles.
Review User Needs Gathering and analyzing data to understand what users require from a product or service to fulfill their goals.
Create User Stories Concise, narrative descriptions of features or functionality from the perspective of an end user.
Site Maps Visual representations of the hierarchical structure and organization of content on a website or application.
Wireframes Low-fidelity, schematic representations of a digital interface, showing layout, content placement, and functionality without detailed design elements.
